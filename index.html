Simon Georges-Kot
Economist and data scientist

I am a researcher in economics and a data scientist at INSEE (Institut National de la Statistique et des Études Économiques), the French statistical office. As an economist, my research interests include labor economics and empirical industrial organization. As a data scientist, I enjoy applying statistical methods to new datasets in order to solve real-world problems. Prior to joining INSEE, I was a student at Ecole Polytechnique, ENSAE and Paris School of Economics with a focus on Economics and Statistics.
